Mobile App Wellness
UI/UX Case Study · Fitness

GentleFit

For beginners · Mood based · Kind motivation

Problem
Most fitness apps are built for experienced users — beginners feel lost, overwhelmed, and quit within days of starting
Solution
A mood-check-in flow that assigns gentle, 5–10 min routines adapted to the user's daily energy level — no equipment, no pressure
Process
Conducted user interviews, built empathy maps, created low-fi wireframes in Miro, then high-fi prototypes in Figma with 2 rounds of usability testing
Result
Improved session completion through gentle habit loops; users reported feeling motivated rather than pressured after each workout session

02 / 03
Mobile App Food & Lifestyle
UI/UX Case Study · Food & Lifestyle

CookedUp

Recipe Discovery · Meal Planning · Food Delivery

Problem
Users had to switch between 3–4 apps to discover recipes, plan meals, and order food — causing frustration and inconsistent eating habits
Solution
A unified food app featuring Tamil Nadu recipes, a smart weekly meal planner, calorie tracking, and integrated food delivery in one seamless flow
Process
Analysed 5 competitor apps (Swiggy, Zomato, Mealime), mapped user flows for 3 key tasks, built component library and delivered full high-fi UI in Figma
Result
Reduced task-switching from 4 apps to 1; clear visual hierarchy and familiar patterns made the app easy to learn for first-time users

Mobile App Time Tracking
UI/UX Case Study · Productivity

See Your Time

Task Management · Time Tracking · Productivity Booster

Problem
Users juggled tasks across notes, calendars and reminders with no single source of truth — leading to missed deadlines and low daily productivity
Solution
A distraction-free dashboard with Pomodoro timer, task priority tags, and auto time-tracking — designed to reduce cognitive load at a glance
Process
Restructured information architecture for clarity, sketched 15+ wireframe screens, validated flows through user walkthroughs, and refined in Figma
Result
Clean visual hierarchy reduced cognitive overload; Pomodoro integration and progress indicators helped users stay consistent and accountable daily
